About Me

Designer. Producer.
Generalist.

My core strength is systems and strategy game design. I think in interconnected mechanics — how a rule at one layer creates interesting decisions three layers deep. I've spent years designing, writing, and shipping complex systems in Paradox's proprietary engine, with a consistent focus on player agency and emergent storytelling.

Beyond design, I spent 18 months as a Producer — a role that sharpened my ability to balance creative ambition with production constraints, communicate across disciplines, and keep large cross-functional teams aligned. I bring that production lens to every design conversation.

I'm a strong writer with native-level English and years of narrative content creation experience. I've written events and systemic content for millions of players, and I care deeply about how language shapes player experience. My talk on CK3's hidden event system was featured in PC Gamer.

On the technical side, I'm comfortable with code — not just engine scripting but genuine programming. My personal projects are built in Unity and Godot where I handle the majority of implementation myself, keeping hands-on skills sharp alongside my design work.
